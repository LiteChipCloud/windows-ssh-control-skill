# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We expect a respectful, professional, and inclusive collaboration environment.

Expected behavior:
1. Discuss problems with evidence and reproducible details.
2. Keep communication respectful and constructive.
3. Assume good intent and avoid personal attacks.

Unacceptable behavior:
1. Harassment, threats, or hate speech.
2. Doxxing or sharing private information.
3. Repeated disruptive behavior in issues/PRs/discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may apply:
1. warning
2. content removal
3. temporary restriction
4. permanent ban in severe or repeated cases

## Reporting

Use private maintainer channels for conduct incidents.
